PORTFOLIO SECTION

Onboarding Sessions: Capture and Tracking Process

Process Summary

The Onboarding Sessions process (also known as Recupero NRT) is designed to contact customers who perform simulations for opening new financial products such as CMR Credit Cards, Checking Accounts and Savings Accounts. This process follows a structured data flow that allows for efficient lead capture and subsequent follow-up.

  • Data origin: Users perform simulations on the bank's website or application from different devices.
  • Initial storage: The data is stored in a specific Rabbit Queue for each country.
  • Processing: The Data Factory processes this data by sending it from Rabbit to Pub/Sub.
  • Transformation: Google Cloud Dataflow performs the processing and transformation of the data.
  • Final storage: The processed data is sent to Cloud Storage and BigQuery.
  • CRM Integration: Simultaneously, the data is sent to a Data Extension in Salesforce.
  • Follow-up: A Journey in Salesforce evaluates specific conditions to send personalized notifications to the customer.
